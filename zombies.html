THE MISSION

Players will embark on a chilling, dark and mysterious journey through a
snowy Bavarian village in Mittelburg, Germany, as they attempt to
recover priceless works of art stolen by the Axis powers in World War II.
This village holds a shadowy secret key to an unimaginable and
monstrous power. Beware of the twisted fate and dire consequences
from the occult horrors awaiting any visitors.
